DOMINO's Article: Data and Software Repository

This page contains the software and the datasets used to accomplish the experiments presented in the paper "Discovering Relaxed Functional Dependencies based on Multi-attribute Dominance", by Loredana Caruccio, Vincenzo Deufemia, Felix Naumann, and Giuseppe Polese, submitted for publication to IEEE TRANSACTIONS ON KNOWLEDGE AND DATA ENGINEERING. This page contains jar files concerning the implementation of DOMINO and the re-implemented versions of DD discovery algorithms presented by Song et al. [1]. The two software require the construction of the in [1] S. Song and L. Chen, “Differential dependencies: Reasoning and discovery,” ACM Transactions on Database Systems (TODS), vol. 36, pp. 16:1–16:41, 2011.
